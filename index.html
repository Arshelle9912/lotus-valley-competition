About Tata Motors

Strengths

It has been earning stabilized profit. Tata has a good management policy. It can be noticed when they acquire new companies. One study showed that Tata will only purchase those companies which have the same management system.

Weaknesses

The automobile industry is a very competitive industry. Every company in this industry remain competitive as most of the automobile companies are very old and experienced in this business. They offer a new model and tech-savvy cars.

Oppurtunities

Modernizing the world. Citizens are heavily reliant on the transport facilities. It’ll boost motor vehicle sales. Seeing that Tata still has the potential to reach another international market, it has a great opportunity to grow.

Threats

Many countries develop carbon emissions laws. If India introduces this kind of law, TATA may need to produce more carbon-efficient cars that may need additional investment.
